# OctoAcme — Stakeholder Engagement Guide

## Purpose
Define how to identify, engage, and maintain alignment with stakeholders throughout the project lifecycle.

## Stakeholder Identification

### When to do this
During project initiation and planning phases.

### Process
1. List all individuals or teams affected by or interested in the project
2. Map each stakeholder to one or more categories:
   - **Decision Maker**: Controls budget, timeline, or scope
   - **Influencer**: Shapes strategy or design decisions
   - **Implementer**: Executes work or delivers components
   - **Affected**: Uses the output or is impacted by changes
   - **Support**: Provides technical, operational, or administrative support

3. Assess engagement level needed:
   - **High**: Frequent communication, regular reviews, early feedback
   - **Medium**: Milestone updates, monthly touchpoints
   - **Low**: Awareness via email or shared dashboards

## Engagement Strategy

### Key Principles
- **Transparency**: Share progress, risks, and decisions openly
- **Timely**: Provide updates before they become surprises
- **Tailored**: Adjust messaging and frequency to stakeholder needs
- **Action-Oriented**: Always include what you need from stakeholders (decisions, input, approval)

### Communication Modes
- **Synchronous**: Kickoff meetings, steering committee meetings, design reviews
- **Asynchronous**: Email updates, shared dashboards, document collaboration
- **Ad-hoc**: Escalation calls for urgent issues or decisions

### Engagement Frequency by Phase

#### **Initiation**
- Kickoff meeting with decision makers and key influencers
- One-pager review and approval
- Expected frequency: 1-2 meetings

#### **Planning**
- Planning review with stakeholders
- Risk and dependency review
- Expected frequency: 1-2 meetings, weekly PM sync

#### **Execution**
- Weekly or bi-weekly status updates (email or dashboard)
- Milestone demos and reviews
- Ad-hoc escalations as needed
- Expected frequency: Weekly email + monthly meeting

#### **Release**
- Pre-release notification and readiness check
- Release announcement with impact summary
- Post-release support coordination
- Expected frequency: 2-3 touchpoints

#### **Retrospective**
- Retrospective debrief with key stakeholders
- Lessons learned and improvement actions
- Expected frequency: 1 meeting
